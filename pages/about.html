About Us

Visual Science was created for eager students wanting to see things in action and teachers in absence of materials to instruct students in an interesting way. It all started with our inability to picture the bland textbook and theories; Science was supposed to be more captivating. Without having the actual tools to provide us with hands-on experiments, we started on this journey to make the textbook alive. Our love and enthusiasm for science drives us to make it elegant for everyone.
